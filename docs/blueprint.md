# **App Name**: VK EcoFlow

## Core Features:

- Dynamic Homepage Display: Render a visually striking homepage with a hero section, animated statistics, services preview, and prominent calls to action.
- Detailed Service & Project Sections: Present comprehensive information for solar, water fountain, and turnkey services, alongside afilterable project gallery featuring case studies.
- Integrated Inquiry & Quote Forms: Provide a streamlined contact form and a 'Get Free Quote' popup to capture user details and service requirements efficiently.
- Responsive Navigation System: Implement a sticky navigation bar that adapts seamlessly across all devices (desktop and mobile) for intuitive user experience.
- Interactive UI Elements: Incorporate smooth micro-interactions, hover effects, and tasteful animations for sections like animated stats and project cards, enhancing user engagement.
- AI-Powered Service Advisor Tool: Utilize a language model to analyze user inputs from the 'Get Free Quote' form and proactively suggest the most suitable service solutions, optimizing lead conversion.
- Essential Business Information Hub: Display critical contact details, a Google Maps integration, working hours, and social media links on a dedicated contact page.

## Style Guidelines:

- The palette is anchored in modern ecological and technological themes, suggesting an innovative yet grounded approach. The scheme favors a predominantly dark interface with vibrant accents. Primary color: A luminous, deep electric blue (#22A7F7) conveys cutting-edge technology and pristine water. Background color: A desaturated, dark grayish-blue (#1C2326) provides a sophisticated and deep foundation. Accent color: A bright, clear aqua-green (#60EDCE) introduces an eco-friendly touch and serves for highlights and interactive elements, ensuring strong contrast against the dark background.
- Headline font: 'Space Grotesk' (sans-serif) for its futuristic and tech-inspired aesthetic, ideal for bold statements. Body font: 'Inter' (sans-serif) provides excellent readability and a clean, neutral feel for longer text blocks, aligning with the minimal design approach.
- Minimalistic line icons with subtle gradients or flat filled icons in accent colors to maintain a clean, futuristic, and high-end corporate aesthetic. Icons should visually represent solar energy, water, and innovation with precision.
- Clean, grid-based layouts with generous spacing and strong visual hierarchy. Elements will feature subtle Glassmorphism and Neumorphism effects, creating depth and a premium feel without sacrificing minimalism. Fully responsive design is prioritized for seamless experience across all device sizes, accompanied by smooth scrolling functionality.
- Sophisticated, smooth transitions and micro-interactions on hover, click, and section scrolls. Animated statistics will dynamically update on view. Unique 'timeline or journey' animations on the About Us page will narrate the company's 15+ years of experience with elegant visual storytelling.
